Frequently Asked

Linear switches have a smooth keystroke with no bump or click. Tactile switches have a noticeable bump mid-press. Clicky switches add an audible click to the bump. Most builders start with linear for gaming and tactile for typing.

Pre-lubed switches like the Gateron Oil King come ready to use. For unlubed switches, hand-lubing with Krytox 205g0 or 3203 significantly improves the sound and feel.

65% boards need around 68, TKL around 87, full-size around 104. Always order 10-15 extra for mistakes or spares.

Unopened switches can be returned within 30 days. Opened or lubed switches are final sale because we cannot resell them once they have been handled.
